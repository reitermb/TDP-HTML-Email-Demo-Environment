Captures the output of dev:design discussions for how to sequence improvements to TDP and Data Submission notification emails.

  • Notify STT Analyst when data for my program has been submitted (currently implemented)
  • Notify STT Analyst when data for my program has been processed and an error report is available (low lift improvement)
    • Adds CTA to sign into TDP and where to seek more guidance in the knowledge center
  • Notify STT Analyst when data for my program has been processed and an error reports are available (higher lift improvement)
    • Adds aggregation to serve up one email informing the user about all submitted sections rather than 1 email per data file.
  • Notify STT Analyst when data for my program has been processed and an error reports are available (higher lift w/ directly linkable quarters)
    • Adds a direct link to the submission history tab of the relevant fiscal year and quarter (requires enhancment to TDP app itself, not just the email notification)
